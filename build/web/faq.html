Frequently Asked Questions

Click "ticket" at the navigation bar. Choose your ticket and the button click book now. Once the button is clicked, it will lead to reserve form to fill in your details. The booking process is done once it is lead to payment page.

For payment, you can have two option either to pay at the counter or pay by debit. In order to make payment, you need to make reservation and it will lead to payment page.

After you have made your reservation, you can check the details by clicking "reserve" at the navigation bar. It will lead you to reservation details page where you can update, delete and edit your details.
