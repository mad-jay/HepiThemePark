About Hepi

Why sad when you can be hepi ? Hepi is a place for you to have fun ! Hepi Theme Park has been listed in the Malaysia Book of Records as the First Pink Theme Park in Malaysia.The enormous pink theme park, which is 150,000 square feet in size, has two thrilling zones: Pop Land for thrill-seekers and Candy Land for children.
